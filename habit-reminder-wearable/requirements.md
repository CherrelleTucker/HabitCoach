# Requirements

## Client Context
Potential client wants a habit-breaking/grounding reminder tool. Primary use case is horse riders who ride alone and want to break bad habits. Secondary applications include physical therapy (posture, form reminders) and general habit/mindfulness training.

## Constraints
- **Budget**: Under $10k (MVP scope)
- **Platform**: iPhone primary, Android secondary
- **Versioning Strategy**: Phone-only MVP first, hardware/wearable support in later versions

## Target Market
- **Primary**: B2C direct-to-consumer via app stores
- **Secondary**: B2B options (therapists, coaches) if feasible

## Desired Features
1. Vibrating/haptic reminders at configurable intervals
2. Intensity variation to mitigate desensitization
3. Verbal commands / voice activation
4. Trigger phrase support
5. Saved session settings (profiles)
6. Affirmations timer
7. Future: dedicated hardware bracelet/wearable

## Known Competitors
- Habit breaker apps exist but with different intentions (need to research specifics)
